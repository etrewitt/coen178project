Students face perennial issues planning and registering for classes, often unable to register for the classes they want due to classes being either too full or simply not offered.

Likewise, faculty have trouble scheduling available classes, frequently having to guess at student interest for courses to plan to offer.

With this tool, students can register interest in future courses, as well as check their current status with their planned and completed courses.

Likewise, faculty can view student interest in each course by year and quarter, and use this tool to plan out their future offerings.
